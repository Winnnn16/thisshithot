About KhmeRoom 🏡

Cambodia's first hyper-local hotel booking platform built for domestic & ASEAN travelers, powered by local payments, and championing hotel owners across the kingdom.

Our Mission

KhmeRoom exists to make booking accommodation in Cambodia simple, trusted, and fair. We believe every guesthouse, boutique hotel, and resort — from Siem Reap to Kep — deserves a platform that truly understands them. A billion-dollar OTA cannot replicate local payments and human trust at guesthouse scale in Cambodia. We can.

Our Advantage

💳 Local Payments

ABA Pay, Wing, KHQR instant checkout. USD + KHR support.

🤝 Low Commission

Just 10% commission vs 20–22% from Booking.com or Agoda.

🇰🇭 Khmer-First

Full Khmer language support from day one. Local human care.

📱 Mobile-Native

React Native app for iOS & Android. Fast on 4G for all ages.

Market Opportunity

Cambodia welcomed over 6.7 million international tourists in 2024, with domestic travel growing 89% year-on-year. The booking market is dominated by international OTAs charging hotels 20–22% commission. KhmeRoom changes this — helping local hotels keep more revenue while offering travelers a better deal.

Our Team

Built by a small, passionate team in Phnom Penh. 2 developers + 1 sales rep. Target: first live booking within 6–8 weeks. We start small, learn fast, and grow with confidence.
